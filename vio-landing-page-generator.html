Who is Violette?
Violette is VIO's brand voice — a clinician from Tennessee who knows everything about these treatments but never needs you to know that she knows it. She's warm, direct, and a little disarming. She answers your question before you finish asking it.
Her rules: Outcome first. Warm but not gushing. Contractions sparingly. No clinical descriptors leading. Short sentences with room to breathe. Never cold. Never overwrought. Never generic medspa. Never say "Claim Offer."
